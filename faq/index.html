Frequently Asked Questions

Products

Are all the products naturally dyed and handwoven?

Yes.

Are these products available in Desi stores as well?

Some of these are available only here, some are available in Desi stores as well.

What is so unique about these products?

These products are made by Charaka Rural Womens' Co-operative in a village in Karnataka. All the products are naturally dyed and handwoven, ensuring they are eco-friendly and handmade.

Are the clothes 100% cotton?

Yes, absolutely.

Do you use organic cotton?

No, we do not.

Are these products Khadi?

No, they are handloom, meaning the weaving process is by hand, whereas the yarn is mill made. For a deeper understanding of handloom, please click here.

Why are only some products listed here and not the complete collection?

We are slowly getting started online. In the next few months, you will see more collections.

Why does the product I ordered home not look exatly like how I saw in the photographs online?

Owing to the quality of naturally dyed fabric, the colours might not render as an exact match in photographs. Moreoever, depending on the natural dye ingredients used, the weather conditions, each batch might vary in their colour shade. Rest assured that the product you are receiving is of good quality and is unique, which is a feature of handmade products.

How can I be sure I am choosing the right size?

If you have not purchased DESI earlier, please feel free to consult our customer care team on Whatsapp before purchase and ask for a size chart.

Will the products shrink after first wash?

Unstitched products like dupatta, stole, towels etc. tend to shrink upto 5%. Garments are pre-shrunk but a slight shrinkage is observed in some cases.

Do I need to starch the clothes?

Not required, the handwoven cotton tends to become softer with each wash. If absolutely required, in case of thinner fabric, a mild starch can be used.

Will the colours fade after washing?

All natural dyed fabrics are prone to bleeding in the first 2-3 washes. The fabric tends to gradually fade over a period of time. Indigo producuts must be dried and stored away from light. Natural fading in places exposed to light as well as at the folds might occur over time.

Exchange and Returns

Can I exchange or return what I purchased?

Exchanges / Returns are entertained in case of damaged products or in case of size mismatch based on availability.

Will I get a replacement if the size I bought doesn’t fit?

Please ensure that you consult us regarding the size before purchase if you have any enquiries. If it still did not fit, you could contact us for an exchange. We will consider it based on availability.

How to send back an item for exchange?

Please ensure that the tag is intact, the bill is enclosed and the product is in new condition (not crumpled, washed, damaged or stained). Pack it well and ship it back to our office address.

Who will bear the cost of sending it back?

You will need to bear the cost of sending it back to us in case of exchange. If returning in case of damage, we will refund the product cost as well as shipping cost.

My package or product is damaged. How to replace it?

Contact us immediately and report the defect with pictures. Ship it back to our office address after confirmation with our Customer Care. Based on availability, we will try to send you the replacement at the earliest.

Can I exchange or return a product I bought online at one of the Desi shops?

Do contact us and we will advise you on the best possible option.

Will I get a refund if I am not satisfied by the product or any exchange options?

Yes, you will be refunded the product amount, but not the shipping cost.
